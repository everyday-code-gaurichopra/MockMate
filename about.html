About MockMate

Our mission is to empower job seekers by providing an accessible, intelligent, and effective tool for interview preparation.

Our Story

MockMate was born from a simple observation: while there are countless resources to find job openings, there's a significant gap in effective, personalized tools for interview practice. Traditional methods like reading articles or practicing in front of a mirror lack the dynamic feedback necessary for real improvement. We wanted to change that.

As students of engineering and technology, we saw an opportunity to leverage the power of artificial intelligence to create a more realistic and beneficial practice environment. We envisioned a platform that could not only ask relevant questions but also provide immediate, constructive criticismâ€”acting as a personal interview coach available 24/7.

What We Do

MockMate simulates real-world job interviews by using cutting-edge AI to:

  • Generate questions tailored to specific job roles and experience levels.
  • Listen to your answers and provide a real-time transcript.
  • Analyze your responses and offer instant feedback on clarity, structure, and content.
  • Provide high-quality example answers to help you understand best practices.

Our goal is to help you walk into your next interview feeling prepared, confident, and ready to make a great impression.

The Team

We are a team of passionate students from Chitkara University, dedicated to using technology to solve real-world problems. This project is a culmination of our studies in front-end development, cloud computing, and artificial intelligence.
