Frequently Asked Questions

Is MockMate free to use?

Yes, this version of MockMate is a project created for educational purposes and is completely free to use.

What technology do you use for the AI?

We use the Google Gemini API for all our artificial intelligence features, including generating interview questions and providing feedback on your answers.

Is my interview data private?

Yes. Your interview data is stored securely in a private collection linked to your user ID in our Firestore database. It is not accessible by other users.

Why do I need to grant camera and microphone access?

Access to your camera and microphone is essential for the core functionality of the app. It allows us to simulate a real video interview and use speech-to-text to transcribe your answers for analysis.

What browsers are supported?

For the best experience, especially for the speech-to-text feature, we recommend using the latest version of Google Chrome. While the site may work on other modern browsers, full functionality is optimized for Chrome.
